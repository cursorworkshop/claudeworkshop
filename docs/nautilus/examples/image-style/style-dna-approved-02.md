# Style DNA (Approved Candidate 02)

This file captures the stylistic properties from the approved look (candidate set A, image 02).

## Core style DNA

- Hand-drawn editorial sketch, dark navy ink on warm peach paper.
- Thin-to-medium, scratchy line quality with visible jitter.
- Moderate local hatching (hoodie/chair/hair), but not full heavy fill.
- Simple geometric monitor/desk shapes with rough hand irregularity.
- One clear focal subject or focal object (human optional).
- Composition may include a monitor, artifacts, or workflow marks depending on scene brief.
- No polished vector edges, no glossy rendering, no photo realism.

## Composition DNA

- Focal subject/object must not dominate frame.
- Keep subject/object relatively small, leaving meaningful negative space.
- Preserve large empty background areas for editorial readability.

## Variation policy

- Preserve style DNA across outputs.
- Vary scene archetype and spatial balance per article:
  - artifact-focused scene
  - workflow-transition scene
  - workspace scene
  - camera distance and orientation
  - prop selection
- Do not clone the exact same framing every time.
